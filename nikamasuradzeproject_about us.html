About Fline

Fline – 84 Years of Excellence in the Automotive World

Founded in 1941, Fline is a legendary automotive brand that has combined power, reliability, and elegance for 84 years. We manufacture sports, regular, and luxury cars, meeting the needs of drivers worldwide—from speed enthusiasts to those who appreciate ultimate comfort.

Every Fline engine is a masterpiece of engineering: powerful, reliable, and long-lasting, delivering outstanding performance and unmatched durability. Our cars dominate the roads, leaving competitors behind not only in speed but also in quality craftsmanship.

Whether it's a high-performance supercar, a luxurious sedan, or a practical city vehicle, Fline remains a symbol of power, style, and cutting-edge technology.
